Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What is your typical process for new projects?

My process typically involves an initial consultation to understand your requirements, followed by a detailed proposal including scope, timeline, and cost estimates. After approval, I move into the design phase, development, testing, and finally deployment with post-launch support.

How long does it take to complete a typical website?

The timeline varies depending on project complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex e-commerce site or custom web application could take 8-12 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during the proposal stage.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and major credit cards. For larger projects, I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and the remaining balance paid upon project completion or in milestone-based installments.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and performance optimization.

Can you work with clients internationally?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. With tools like Zoom, Slack, and email, communication is seamless regardless of location. I'm also flexible with scheduling meetings across different time zones.
