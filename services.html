Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about my services

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, scope, and requirements. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects like e-commerce sites or custom applications typically take 6-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific needs.

What is your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit to begin the project, with the remaining 50% due upon completion. For larger projects, I can establish a milestone-based payment schedule. All payment terms are clearly outlined in the project proposal and contract.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer various maintenance and support packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Each package comes with different levels of service based on your needs.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in a wide range of modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Angular, Vue.js), Node.js, PHP, and various CMS platforms like WordPress. For blockchain development, I work with Ethereum, Solidity, Web3.js, and other related technologies. I'm always learning and adapting to new technologies to provide the best solutions.

Do you provide content creation services?

While I primarily focus on the technical aspects of web and blockchain development, I can recommend trusted partners for content creation, copywriting, and photography needs. For projects requiring extensive content creation, I can coordinate with these partners to ensure a seamless process.

How do we get started working together?

The process begins with an initial consultation where we discuss your project requirements, goals, and timeline. After gathering all necessary information, I'll provide a detailed proposal including scope, timeline, and pricing. Once approved, we'll sign a contract and begin the discovery phase of the project.
